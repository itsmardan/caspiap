Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Caspia Research

Introduction

This Privacy Policy reflects our commitment to safeguarding privacy and handling personal information with the highest level of care and confidentiality. At Caspia Research, we provide tailored software solutions and digital marketing services. This document outlines our practices regarding the management and protection of personal data.

Use of Information

We use personal information exclusively to deliver customized software and digital marketing solutions to our clients. Our goal is to meet our clients’ specific requirements, ensuring that the data we collect is relevant and necessary for the services we provide.

Data Confidentiality and Security

The confidentiality and security of our clients’ data are paramount. All information is stored within a highly secure cloud infrastructure, fortified with advanced security measures such as sophisticated firewalls. This security protocol is designed to protect data from unauthorized access and breaches.

Data Collection Limitations

We are committed to limiting our data collection to only what is essential for conducting our business effectively. We specifically avoid gathering or storing sensitive personal identification information beyond what is necessary for our operations.

World-Class Data Security Measures

Our approach to data security includes implementing the highest standard of protective measures, such as robust firewalls and other state-of-the-art technologies. We are dedicated to creating and maintaining a secure data environment for our clients.

Data Retention Policy

Our data retention strategy is minimalist, focusing on holding data no longer than necessary. Sensitive information is not retained, and we favor digital transactions to minimize data storage. For payments, we recommend BACS transfers or using a secure payment link, ensuring no transaction data is stored on our systems.

User Rights

In accordance with the GDPR and UK Data Protection Act, individuals possess rights over their data, including access, correction, and deletion requests. Any inquiries or requests regarding personal data can be addressed through the contact details provided below.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may periodically update this Privacy Policy. The latest version will always be accessible at https://caspia.co.uk/about-us/privacy-policy/. We encourage individuals to review this policy frequently to remain informed about how we protect personal information.
